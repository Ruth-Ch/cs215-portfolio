##  Final Project Ideas

### 1. Evaluating Large Language Models
This project will compare different LLMs across tasks such as reasoning, factual recall, and coding.

**Research Questions:**
- How do different LLMs compare in accuracy and response time?
- Are LLM outputs consistent across repeated prompts?
- Do certain models perform better on specific task types?

---
### 2. Tech Job Market & Layoff Trends
This project explores trends in layoffs and hiring in the tech industry.

**Research Questions:**
- What trends exist in tech layoffs over time?
- Are layoffs correlated with company size or industry?
- What skills are in demand after layoffs?

---
### 3. Understanding How People Use AI Tools
This project analyzes how people interact with LLMs and what makes prompts effective.

**Research Questions:**
- What types of tasks do users most commonly use AI tools for?
- How does prompt complexity affect response quality?
- What patterns distinguish effective vs ineffective prompts?
